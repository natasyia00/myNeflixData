Analyze the variables & cleaning the data

After loading the dataset into a dataframe, I analyzed the data and do some cleaning to ensure there are no null values, duplicates and remove any unecessary columns. After cleaning, the dataset have a total of 2754 rows and 15 columns. Below are the specific variables that will be used for the further exploratory data analysis down below:

  • Type: Differentiate between TV shows and movies to examine the balance between episodic and standalone content.
  • Title + Episode: Show specific episodes and titles to identify popular and recurring content.
  • Title Name: Show the overall content profile.
  • Show Country: Geographical origin of content to identify preferences for specific regions.
  • Start Time: Starting time to watch the content.
  • Duration: Duration of the content viewing.
  • Genres: Types of sub-genres of the content.
  • Parent Genre: Overall, overarching genre categories to discern broader content preferences.
